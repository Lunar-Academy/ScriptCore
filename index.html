Mission

Scriptcore is an organization brought up with the intent of bringing computer science education for all levels, equipping participants with essential skills for various industries and cultivating valuable skills.

+ Students Taught

Through this, we've taught 200+ students, and are looking forward to expanding further to more online classes, more districts, and more courses.
